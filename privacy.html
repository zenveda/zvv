Data Consent & Messaging Policy

At Project Origins, we believe in full transparency and compliance with data privacy standards and messaging regulations.

In accordance with industry standards and compliance frameworks, we collect proof of consent from all users and consumers before initiating any marketing or transactional communication.

This proof of consent may take the form of:

  • A URL link to a webpage where consent is explicitly provided by the consumer
  • A hosted image file demonstrating opt-in consent via screenshot
  • A hosted document outlining the context and method of the opt-in event

We support the submission of multiple URLs that collectively demonstrate the story of the opt-in. All URLs must be publicly accessible, resolvable, and compliant with platform policies.

Our agency maintains a secure, timestamped audit trail for each consent record. These records can be made available to relevant authorities or platforms upon request to ensure full compliance.

By engaging with Project Origins, you acknowledge and agree that any messaging communications we initiate will only occur upon lawful and recorded consent.
